Setup & Integration Guide

  1. Installation Location:
    Ensure the three folders (msdconverter/, ManiaKeystrokes/, and HitCounter/) are placed inside tosu's static/ directory:
    tosu/static/
    ├── msdconverter/ (contains oris-mania-utils.exe)
    ├── ManiaKeystrokes/
    └── HitCounter/
  2. Configuration:
    Set your osu! Songs folder path in the Manager panel and click Save.
  3. Enable Tosu In-Game Overlay:
    Open http://127.0.0.1:24050/settings in your browser and verify that In-Game Overlay is enabled (toggled ON).
  4. Add Overlays inside osu!:
    Launch osu! and make sure Tosu is running. Press the overlay toggle keybind (default: Ctrl + Shift + Space or Ctrl + Shift + ~) to open Tosu's menu. Right-click anywhere in-game and choose Add Overlay to add them one by one.
  5. Browser / OBS Sources:
    To capture them in OBS or open them in your browser, use these local URLs:
    • MSD Radar: http://localhost:24050/msdconverter/
    • Keystrokes: http://localhost:24050/ManiaKeystrokes/
    • Hit Counter: http://localhost:24050/HitCounter/
    (Click any link above to copy and open in browser)
